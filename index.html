Project Overview

A WordPress plugin that creates an educational resource using MkDocs. This project provides a set of markdown files (starting with glossary.md) to help clarify, educate, and elucidate AI terminology, benefits, risks, and strategies. An AI literacy resource aimed at making AI concepts accessible and understandable.

ðŸŽ¯ Vision

Create a comprehensive, accessible resource that demystifies artificial intelligence for everyone. Whether you're a business leader evaluating AI solutions, a developer building AI-powered applications, or someone curious about AI's impact on society, this resource provides clear, jargon-free explanations of AI concepts.

Our Goals:

  • Clarify complex AI terminology into understandable language
  • Educate on both the capabilities and limitations of AI technologies
  • Elucidate the real-world benefits, risks, and ethical considerations
  • Empower informed decision-making about AI adoption and usage
  • Build a foundation for AI literacy that grows with the technology

This is a living resource that will evolve as AI technology advances, ensuring the content remains relevant and practical.
